Cloudiness vs. Latitude



Observations


Clouds, clouds, clouds. This graph shows the cloudiness at many cities around the world. There is a cluster of cities with little to no cloud cover distributed fairly evenly across all latitudes. It must be a nice day in those cities although there will be no cloud watching and fun shape searching. Too bad, those people will have to find another way to pass the time
